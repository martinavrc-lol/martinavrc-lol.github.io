about/

And Marketing is a fictional full-service marketing agency — my very first WordPress project ever. Inspired by 50s advertising aesthetics, yellow pages, and bold print design, it was my first attempt at building a complete professional site from scratch.

problems/

Starting from zero, the challenge was learning the full WordPress workflow: setting up pages, navigation, Elementor layouts, multilingual content, and a coherent visual identity — all at the same time, with no prior experience.

solutions/

I built a complete agency site with a services section, a projects portfolio, an about page, client testimonials, and a contact form. The design draws from retro print culture — high contrast, bold type, structured layouts. A real exercise in learning by doing.
